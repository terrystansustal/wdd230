Site Purpose

The purpose of this website is to provide the latest information of Daly City, California. It will also be a resource that's available online for local business owners that are planning to expand their business and to promote the area. It will also attract people, tourism, and additional business. In addition, the website will include networking events, local charity events, educational opportunities, and many more.
