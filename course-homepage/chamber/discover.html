Fun Places to Explore!

Activity

If you love nature or enjoy walking around with your family and friends, Daly City offers 11 scenic hiking trails! If you like hiking by the beach, mountains, or in the woods we got it all for you!

Beach

Hundreds of people enjoy the beach in the Bay Area! In Daly City, we are near the Ocean Beach and the water is the body of the Pacific Ocean! Many people come by here every day to walk their dogs, surf, and even have a bon fire! Do you love going to the beach? We got you covered!

Restaurants

Daly City is a diverse city! We have a handfull of restaurants in different cultures. If you are interested to try food from other culture Daly City is the right spot to go to! Our well-known restaurants here are Jollibee, Original Joe's Westlake, Boulevard Cafe, Lisa's Mexican Restaurant, and many more!

Population

Daly City is a growing city! According to Census, the population estimate of July 2021 is 101,243! The city is very diverse. The citizens here are 57.3% Asian, 23.1% Hispanic or Latino, 19.2% Caucasian, and 3.5% African American.

School

Do you have a family member that needs to go to school? Daly City offers 22 public schools serving 10,077 students and 6 private schools serving 1,279 students. The school district in this area is under Jefferson Union High School District.
