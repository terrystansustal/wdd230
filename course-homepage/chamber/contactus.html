Myka Winter is the President of the Chamber of Commerce in Daly City, CA, where she has been actively involved for the past 10 years. She owns and operates a successful marketing agency that specializes in helping small businesses increase their online presence. In her free time, Myka enjoys hiking in the nearby hills, trying out new recipes, and volunteering at a local animal shelter.

John Henrie is the Secretary of the Chamber of Commerce in Daly City, CA, where he has been an active member for over 5 years. He runs his own accounting firm that caters to local businesses in the area. In his free time, John enjoys playing basketball with his friends, attending local concerts, and reading non-fiction books on personal finance.

Michael Stevenson is the Chairman of the Chamber of Commence in Daly City, CA, where he has been an active member for 6 years. He owns a gas station that serves the community in the area. When John is not busy, he enjoys going to the movie theater, going out with his family for a quick trip, and fishing with his friends somewhere at Halfmoon Bay, CA.
